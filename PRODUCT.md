# Product

## Register

product

## Users
School Administrators, Teachers, Students, and Parents. The platform serves educational institutions and corporate training environments.

## Product Purpose
A production-grade Smart Attendance and Absence Monitoring SaaS. It aims to automate attendance tracking via dynamic QR codes, manage justification workflows, and provide real-time analytics for risk detection.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Minimalist, and Professional. The "Notion Light" aesthetic (Clean white surfaces, subtle grays, and precise typography) conveys a sense of clarity, reliability, and academic excellence.

## Anti-references
- Clunky legacy ERP/SIS software.
- Overly dark or "Cyber" aesthetics that feel too aggressive for an academic environment.
- Generic "SaaS-cream" templates with excessive pastel gradients.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity through Minimalism**: Use white space and subtle borders to create a focused, low-distraction environment.
- **Typographic Hierarchy**: Rely on high-quality typography (Inter/NotionInter) to establish structure and importance.
- **Functional Surfaces**: Use light gray surfaces and subtle shadows to indicate depth and interactivity.
- **Contextual Actions**: Prioritize clean, accessible controls that feel native to the educational workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target: WCAG 2.1 AA.
- High contrast for text elements.
- Screen-reader friendly components for student and parent accessibility.
